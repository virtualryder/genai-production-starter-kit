ACME Corp — Employee Policies (Sample Document)

Remote Work Policy
------------------
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with prior manager approval.
All remote work requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 48 hours in advance.
Employees are expected to be available during core hours: 10 AM – 3 PM local time.

Work Hours
----------
Standard work week is 40 hours.
Overtime must be pre-approved by a manager.
Time tracking is required for all hourly employees.

Data Security
-------------
Employees must use company-approved VPN when accessing internal systems remotely.
Personal devices are not permitted to store company data without IT approval.
All sensitive documents must be encrypted at rest.
