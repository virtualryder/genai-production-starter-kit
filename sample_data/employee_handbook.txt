SECTION 1 — EMPLOYMENT POLICIES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1.1 Work Hours
Standard work week is 40 hours, Monday through Friday.
Core hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone.
Flexible start and end times are permitted outside core hours with manager agreement.
Overtime must be pre-approved in writing by a direct manager.

1.2 Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with prior manager approval.
All remote work requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 48 hours in advance.
Employees working remotely must be available via Slack and email during core hours.
Remote work from outside the employee's home country requires a separate international remote work request and may have tax and legal implications.

1.3 Time Off and Leave
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, accrued monthly.
Unused PTO rolls over up to a maximum of 10 days at the end of the calendar year.
Sick leave is separate from PTO: employees receive 10 sick days per year, non-accruing.
Parental leave: 16 weeks fully paid for primary caregivers, 6 weeks for secondary caregivers.
Bereavement leave: 5 days for immediate family, 2 days for extended family.
All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal with at least 2 weeks notice where practicable.

1.4 Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted twice per year: in June and December.
Employees and managers set OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) at the start of each half.
Ratings: Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement.
Compensation adjustments tied to performance reviews are effective the first of the following month.

SECTION 2 — COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1 Salary and Pay
Salaries are paid bi-weekly via direct deposit.
Pay stubs are available in the employee portal.
Annual cost-of-living increases are reviewed in December and effective January 1.

2.2 Health Benefits
ACME Corp covers 100% of employee health, dental, and vision premiums.
Dependents may be added to health plans; ACME covers 60% of dependent premiums.
Open enrollment runs each November for coverage starting January 1.
Employees may elect an HSA-compatible high-deductible plan; ACME contributes $750/year to the HSA.

2.3 Retirement
401(k) plan available to all employees after 60 days of employment.
ACME matches 100% of contributions up to 4% of base salary.
Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years.

2.4 Learning & Development
Each employee receives a $2,000 annual learning and development budget.
Budget resets on January 1 and does not roll over.
Eligible expenses: conferences, online courses, books, certifications.
Reimbursement requires manager approval and a receipt; submit via the expense portal within 60 days.

2.5 Equipment and Home Office
New employees receive a laptop and standard peripherals upon joining.
A one-time $500 home office stipend is available after 90 days; submit receipts via the expense portal.
Equipment remains property of ACME Corp and must be returned upon separation.

SECTION 3 — CODE OF CONDUCT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1 Professional Behavior
All employees are expected to treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect.
Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation of any kind are grounds for immediate termination.
Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR in writing within 30 days of arising.

3.2 Confidentiality
Employees must not share proprietary business information, customer data, or unreleased product details outside ACME without explicit written authorization.
NDAs are in effect for the duration of employment and for 2 years post-separation.

3.3 Social Media
Employees may discuss their work publicly but must not share confidential information, financial projections, or customer names without consent.
When posting about ACME, employees should clarify they are sharing personal views, not official company positions.

SECTION 4 — SEPARATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 Voluntary Resignation
Employees are asked to provide a minimum of 2 weeks written notice.
Final paycheck, including accrued unused PTO, is issued on the next regular pay date.
Equipment must be returned within 5 business days of last day.

4.2 Involuntary Termination
ACME Corp is an at-will employer except where prohibited by law.
Severance packages, where offered, are detailed in the individual separation agreement.
